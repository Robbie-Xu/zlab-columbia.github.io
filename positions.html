Open Positions

We are looking for highly motivated students and postdocs to join our research team.


Postdocs: please e-mail your statement of interest, CV, and three recommendation letters.


Graduate students: if you have been admitted to Columbia, e-mail your graduate grades and qualifying exam status, and arrange to visit our lab; if you have not been admitted, please apply to the graduate program.


Undergraduate students: please e-mail a brief statement of interest, CV/resume, your grades, and whether you require financial support. (Currently there are no high school student positions.)
